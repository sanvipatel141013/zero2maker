About Steve Jobs

Steven Paul Jobs (1955–2011) was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and business magnate. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc., and is widely recognized as one of the greatest visionaries in the history of technology.

Personal Details

Born: February 24, 1955

Major Achievements

  • Co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976
  • Revolutionized personal computing with the Macintosh
  • Transformed music with the iPod and iTunes
  • Redefined smartphones with the iPhone
  • Introduced the iPad, changing digital media consumption
  • Helped turn Pixar into a world-class animation studio
